About SPARQ

If crunching numbers and probability puzzles sound like something that floats your boat, this event is for you! Put your skills in data analysis, coding and quantitative trading to the test in this thrilling competition.

SPARQ is a cutting-edge technical event under the INTEGRATION banner of Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata. With a focus on statistics, probability, analysis, and quantitative research, SPARQ aims to ignite curiosity and foster data-driven problem-solving.
